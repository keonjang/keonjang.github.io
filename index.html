Keon Jang

I am a Software Engineer at Google Networking Infrastructure Team since 2016. From 2014 to 2015, I was a Research Scientist at Intel Labs. From 2012 to 2014, I was a Post-Doctoral Researcher at Microsoft Research Cambridge, UK. I received Ph.D. in Computer Science at KAIST in 2012 under Sue Moon and KyoungSoo Park.
